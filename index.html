Data Transfer

Export a backup file on one device, then import it on another to sync your stats + spaced-rep progress.

Every
Auto-save runs silently in the background whenever your stats change (no popups). You still can manually Save/Load above.
Tip: importing replaces the current device’s saved data with what’s inside the file.

Cloud Sync (Username)

Sync your progress between phone and computer using a simple username. This uses a free Firebase backend (you’ll paste your Firebase config once).

Tip: use a short code you’ll remember. Without a code, anyone who guesses your username could overwrite your data.
“Save” uploads your current device data. “Load” replaces this device’s data with the cloud copy, then reloads.
